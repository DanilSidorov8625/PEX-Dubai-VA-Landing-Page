What Is Shop & Ship?

Shop & Ship (also known as parcel forwarding) lets you buy from U.S. and Canadian stores that don't ship to the UAE.

Polar Express gives you a personal U.S. and Canadian address. We receive your purchases, prepare them securely, and forward them straight to your door anywhere in the Emirates.
